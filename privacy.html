🔒 Privacy Policy

Keep My GHL Chrome Extension  ·  Last updated: June 2026

Short version: Keep My GHL runs entirely in your browser. We never collect, transmit, or store your data on any server. Everything stays on your device.

What We Access

The extension requests access to the following browser capabilities:

  • Active Tab — to read the page you are currently viewing in GoHighLevel AI Studio
  • Scripting — to inject content scripts that extract page HTML, CSS, JS, and assets
  • Downloads — to save ZIP files to your computer
  • Storage — to save your API tokens (GitHub, Netlify, Vercel, Cloudflare) and snapshots locally in your browser
  • Web Navigation — to identify page frames for accurate content extraction
  • Tabs — to find open GHL tabs for auto-backup scheduling
  • Alarms — to run scheduled auto-backups at the interval you choose
  • All URLs — to fetch external CSS, JS, fonts, and images referenced by your GHL pages so they can be bundled into self-contained exports

What We Do NOT Do

  • We do not collect any personal information
  • We do not transmit your page content, credentials, or snapshots to any server we operate
  • We do not use analytics or tracking of any kind
  • We do not share data with third parties
  • We do not read or access any pages outside of GoHighLevel / LeadConnector domains (except to fetch assets referenced by those pages)

Where Your Data Goes

When you use the extension's deploy features, files are sent directly from your browser to the service you choose:

  • GitHub — your token and files go directly to api.github.com
  • Netlify — your token and files go directly to api.netlify.com
  • Vercel — your token and files go directly to api.vercel.com
  • Cloudflare Pages — your token and files go directly to api.cloudflare.com

These requests are made by your browser under your credentials. Keep My GHL acts as a bridge — it never sees or stores the response on our end because there is no "our end."

API Token Storage

API tokens you enter (GitHub PAT, Vercel token, Netlify token, Cloudflare API token) are stored in chrome.storage.local — this is local-only storage on your device, encrypted by Chrome, and never synced to Chrome's cloud sync.

To remove stored tokens at any time, go to chrome://extensions → Keep My GHL → Clear storage, or use the Settings panel inside the extension.

Version History & Snapshots

When auto-backup is enabled or you manually grab a project, snapshots are stored in chrome.storage.local on your device. They are not uploaded anywhere unless you explicitly click a deploy button.

Permissions Justification

The <all_urls> host permission is required to fetch external assets (CSS, JS, fonts, images) that GHL pages load from CDNs. Without this, exports would be broken with missing styles and scripts. The extension only fetches URLs that are already referenced on the page you are exporting.

Children's Privacy

This extension is intended for web developers and digital marketers. It is not directed at children under 13 and we do not knowingly collect information from children.

Changes to This Policy

If this policy changes materially, we will update the "Last updated" date above and note the change in the extension's release notes.
